Time for Izmir

Incredible Shopping Spots

Izmir is one of the world's great shopping destinations for tourists. The city has plenty of shopping places where you can find great products which will satisfy the shopper in you. Izmir has been a magnet for merchants purveying in the exotic and wonderful, and those in search of retail therapy. Shopping in Izmir can be a very nice experience for those who have never been here before.

Kemeralti

The trade center at the western end of the historical Silk Road used to come up to the port in Izmir. The goods brought to Izmir by camel caravans following the Silk Road were unloaded to these inns and exported from the port to ships by Genoese merchants.

Kizlaragasi

One of the important buildings for Izmir is Kızlarağası Inn. it is one of the rare works of Ottoman architecture in Izmir, whose construction was completed in 1744. The inn near the Izmir Harbor Castle has played an important role in the city's trade since its completion.

Konak Pier

Konak Pier was designed in 1890 by the famous French architect and construction engineer Gustave Eiffel, to function as a customs building on the shore of the Izmir Bay.
