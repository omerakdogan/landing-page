Time for Izmir

Discover the Beaches

Izmir, the heart of the Aegean, has many beautiful seaside towns where you can swim in azure waters of the Aegean Sea such as an aquarium, live in peace and enjoy fresh seafood. These towns along the shore offer all the blessings of nature to their guests and make people happy. Every corner of Izmir is beautiful from the north to the south, but we think that some places are more beautiful. Izmir has 47 blue flag beaches and the best surf spots in the world. Here I have listed the 4 most cool beach towns near Izmir for you. Also, in order to speed up your holiday plans,I would like to share the range of monthly water temperatures at touristic season in Izmir, where you can swim in every season except winter.

Cesme

Cesme is a wonderful one for those who love the sea, sun, beach with 30 km long coastline. This gorgeous coastal town is famous for its colourful nightlife, windsurf centers, golden sandy beaches.

Alacati

Alacatiis a very ideal spot for windsurfing. This lovely town fascinates visitors with its famous herb festival, blue-white stone houses, beautiful streets adorned with bougainvillea, colourful boutique hotels, windmills, shops, cafes and chic restaurants.

Urla

This lovely seaside town, which is among the closest holiday places to Izmir, is one of the best places for those who want to escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Foca

Foca is famous for its narrow streets, windmills, seals, stray cats and traditional stone houses.
