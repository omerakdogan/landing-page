Time for Izmir

Historical Places in Izmir

With its outstanding history, world famous antique cities and unique historical buildings, Izmir is a real Aegean legend. Izmir, a city dating back 8500 years, has hosted a number of different civilizations like the Hittites, the Greek City-states the Romans, the Byzantines, the Seljuq Empire and the Ottoman Empire.

Ephesus

This mega ancient city has been standing for thousands of years with its world famous architectural monuments.One of the most striking features of Ephesus is that all avenues and streets intersect each other at right angles.

Pergamon

Pergamon Ancient City was one of the oldest settlements in the history of civilization with a history of 8,500 years BC. It is important as the cultural, scientific and artistic center , having the most impressive monumental works of the Hellenistic Roman and Byzantine periods. It has the first major hospital in history called Asklepion.

Clock Tower

The Clock Tower, which decorates Konak Square, is an art monument.It was built of white marble in terms of the body, with the remaining parts of cut stone carvings in 1901.

Elevator

The purpose of the historical elevator is to eliminate the need for the people to climb the stairs of 155 steps and to make their lives easier. It was built in 1907.
